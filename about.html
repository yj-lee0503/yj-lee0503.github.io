Yong-Jin Lee #

Committed Husband Distinguished Data Engineer Veteran Software Engineer Perpetual Learner Creative Problem-Solver Global Traveler Passionate Reader

Professional Trajectory & Educational Accomplishments

Fueled by an enduring energy for problem-solving, I approach each new challenge as an opportunity for growth, consistently honing my skills to navigate complexities. My professional journey, having led me through the intricate realm of data - from analytics to engineering - has proven to be a deeply satisfying intellectual adventure.

The genesis of my academic pursuits took place at the University of Virginia, where I found myself irresistibly drawn to the analytical rigor of Mathematics. This attraction wasn't founded on a clear career trajectory but rather on a genuine love for problem-solving and a conviction that a mathematical foundation would serve me well in diverse scenarios. This belief was confirmed when I landed my initial role in a renowned data company, kindling a lifelong passion for data manipulation - an engaging, ever-changing puzzle I solve daily. This journey has offered me extensive experience and honed my abilities as a data analyst, BI developer, and data engineer.

Driven by this passion, I secured a graduate certificate in Data Science from George Mason University. Moreover, I completed my Masters in Computer and Information Technology from the University of Pennsylvania in December 2022 with great pride. Continual learning, a foundational element of my personal and professional development, propels me forward. Armed with these qualifications and rich experience, my mission is to enable a broader audience to harness the power of data, transforming it from a daunting entity into a potent, user-friendly resource.
